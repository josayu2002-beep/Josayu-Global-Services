About Josayu Global

Registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (RC: 1190641) and operating with full regulatory compliance.

Excellence in Engineering & Energy

Established in 2010, Josayu Global Services has grown into a trusted name in Nigeria's infrastructure sector. We are a registered limited liability company dedicated to providing innovative, sustainable, and cost-effective solutions in electrical engineering, renewable energy, and general construction.

Our expertise extends to sourcing high-quality equipment, including BlueGate inverters and batteries, ensuring our clients receive premium products backed by our installation and after-sales support.
